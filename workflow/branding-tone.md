# 🎨 Branding & Tone Guide

Wi-Fi Kickstart isn’t just a diagnostic tool—it’s a voice of clarity in a noisy digital world. Here’s how we speak:

## 🗽 NYC Identity
- Confident, direct, and unapologetically helpful
- Grit meets polish—every message should feel earned

## 💬 Diagnostic Copy
- Avoid jargon unless it empowers
- Use metaphors sparingly, but clearly (“Your Wi-Fi’s stuck in traffic”)
- Always offer next steps or emotional reassurance

## 🧠 Support Tone
- Empathetic, not robotic
- “We’ve got your back” > “Please try again”
- Celebrate user wins (“Connection restored—nice work!”)

## 🧩 Popup UX Voice
- Modular, minimal, and emotionally clear
- Every label should feel like a helpful nudge, not a lecture
